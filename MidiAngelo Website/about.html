Features of this website
Note: This is just the features implemented so far. We will have more in the future. Stay tuned!

Making music from drawing

You can draw what you want on the canvas and can listen to your art. Our website does some sorcery to convert your drawing to music!

Choosing instruments that play over the .midi file

We have a library of instruments for you to select from and this includes but not limited to string, woodwind, brass, drums, rtc.

A big drawing canvas is your playground

Draw whatever you can imagine on the canvas provided. Feel the canvas is too small? No problem! Increase the canvas size as you see fit.

Track and check your work over time

Login and see what you have created so far and how you have improved over time

Convert .midi files to .mp3

You don't feel like drawing but you have a .midi file that you want to convert to mp3? Come right in! Our website can do that in no time.

Choose your bpm

You have made your song but feel the track is either slow or fast? Don't worry. With our bpm selector, you can input the bpm you want and change the speed of the music.

Organize contests

You can host competitions to see who comes up with the best art and music by setting up a leaderboard and letting people vote on the submissions you get on your contest.

Know where you stand in your community

You can click on a user profile to see their gallery and their submissions in the contests they have participated in.

Comment your thoughts on others work

Want to be a critic? There is a comment box under a painting-mp3 combo, where you can leave your thoughts for the artist to see!

Login to save your work periodically

(Feature not implemented)You can create an account and login to save your work periodically so that you dont lose any of your precious time or imagination you brought to life in your art.
